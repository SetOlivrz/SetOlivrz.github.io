House of Chores and Tours

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

A first person simulation game where you take the role of a kid who needed to do chores clean up the house before your parents gets home. Made under the Game Jam theme of "What Feels Like Home to You".

This project was one of our outputs in our GDFUNDA (Game Fundamentals), where we are tasked to create and apply custom materials in our very own level design.
