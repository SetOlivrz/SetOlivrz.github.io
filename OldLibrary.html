Old Library

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

A virtual space made for AltSpace VR. The design was inspired by the interior and ambiance of the Dawn Winery in Genshin impact. All assets in the space such as models, light and particles are made in Unity Engine while the materials are tailored from mixing and matching of various PBS textures.

ROLE AND CONTRIBUTIONS

In this project, the whole team worked as a game level designer where we are to create models from scratch and set up the whole scene. In this project, I primarily worked on the following:

  • 3D modeling using Unity ProBuilder: foundation of the room and furniture
  • Creating materials from various textures
  • Room setup
  • Lighting setup
  • Fixing Artifacts
  • Animating
